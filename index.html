Our Origin

A Name Born From Resilience

Elrisa takes its name from a story of hope. Our founder's daughter, Elsa, was born during a season of uncertainty — her arrival against all odds became a symbol of life pushing through pressure, of strength rising naturally from constraint.

That spirit lives in everything we do. We see potential where others see waste. We extract value from what the world discards. We believe the most powerful transformations begin under pressure.

What We Do

From Raw Residue to Industrial Value

We operate across the full value chain — from field sourcing of agricultural residues to the production of specialty chemicals used in industries worldwide.

Extraction

Sourcing and extracting CNSL, phenolic derivatives, and bio-oils from cashew nut shells and other agricultural residues.

Processing

Refining, modifying, and compounding renewable raw materials into high-performance chemical intermediates.

Manufacturing

Producing polyols, resins, adhesives, coatings, and performance materials for industrial applications.

Global Trade

Exporting and distributing raw materials, intermediates, and finished chemical products to international markets.

Our Process

From Shell to Specialty Chemical

A vertically integrated approach that captures value at every stage of the transformation.

1

Source

We partner directly with cashew processors and farmers across Ghana's Bono region to source raw shells with intact CNSL oil.

2

Extract

Cashew nut shell liquid is carefully extracted using methods that preserve the valuable phenolic compounds within.

3

Refine

Raw CNSL undergoes processing, modification, and formulation to create specialty chemical intermediates.

4

Deliver

Finished products are distributed to industrial customers across sectors from automotive to construction worldwide.
