The Process

When We Hire,
Here's How It Works

We respect candidates' time. When a role opens, here's exactly what to expect — no guessing, no waiting in the dark.

1

Application Received

You'll get an acknowledgment email within 48 hours of sending your application.

2

CV & Cover Letter Review

Our R&D Director personally reviews each application — no automated screening filters.

3

Initial Conversation

A 30-minute call to learn about you, discuss the role, and answer your questions honestly.

4

Technical Discussion

A deeper conversation about your technical background and how it maps to our R&D direction.

5

Offer & Onboarding

If it's a match on both sides, we move quickly to an offer and get you settled in.

Application Tips

How to Stand Out

  • Tell us specifically which polymer systems or chemistries you have hands-on experience with — names of resin types, methods used, or products you helped develop.
  • Show us you're curious. Mention what drew you to bio-based or specialty chemistry, even if you're early in your career.
  • Your cover letter doesn't have to be formal. Write like you're talking to a colleague — be direct about why you want this specific role at this specific company.
  • Entry-level welcome. If you're 0 years post-graduation with a strong academic chemistry background, apply. We care about aptitude and attitude.

Include in your application: Your CV / resume, a cover letter (even a brief one), and any relevant project work, thesis, or publications if available.
