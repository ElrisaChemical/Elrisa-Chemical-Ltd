Ghana Advantage

Why Source CNSL from Ghana?

Ghana is one of West Africa's principal cashew producers, with significant processing capacity in the Bono region. CNSL is a direct co-product of cashew nut processing — sourcing from Ghana means traceability from farm to export, reliable volumes aligned to the annual cashew harvest, and competitive pricing relative to other CNSL-producing regions.

Ghana also offers good port infrastructure for containerised chemical exports through Tema, west of Accra — one of West Africa's largest deep-water ports — with established shipping routes to European, American, and Asian destinations.

Markets

Continents We Export To

We export to industrial buyers across these regions and actively welcome new B2B relationships. If you are sourcing from any of these markets, we want to hear from you.

🇺🇸

North America

North America is our primary target export market. We are actively reaching friction material manufacturers, bio-based chemical formulators, and specialty chemical distributors in the US and Canada who need a reliable West African CNSL source with full export documentation capabilities.

🇪🇺

Europe

European buyers are driven by REACH compliance and bio-based content requirements. We export CNSL, cardanol, and resins to buyers across the continent — Germany, the Netherlands, Italy, and beyond — where demand for phenol-free alternatives and documented bio-based sourcing is growing steadily.

🌏

Asia

India, South Korea, and Japan are established markets for CNSL in friction materials and coatings. We export to buyers across Asia who are looking for a reliable African supply complement to diversify their existing CNSL sourcing from India and Southeast Asia.

🌍

West Africa

Regional buyers across West Africa benefit from shorter lead times and simpler logistics. We export to chemical formulators and manufacturers in the region who need bio-based inputs for locally produced goods — Côte d'Ivoire, Benin, Nigeria, and beyond.

🇬🇧

United Kingdom

The UK is a key target market for Elrisa. We are actively pursuing relationships with specialty chemical distributors, friction material manufacturers, and bio-materials researchers seeking a direct Ghana supply route for CNSL-based products.

🌐

Other Markets

How an Export Enquiry Works

We keep it straightforward. Most initial enquiries receive a response within two business days.

1

Send Your Enquiry

Use the contact form or email us directly. Tell us which product you need, your approximate volume requirements, your destination country, and any technical specification requirements. The more detail you provide, the faster we can respond usefully.

2

We Send Specifications & Pricing

We respond with the relevant product specifications, available grades, and export pricing for your destination. If your enquiry involves a custom formulation, we may ask a few clarifying questions before quoting.

3

Sample or Commercial Order

For new relationships, we are happy to arrange sample quantities before committing to a full commercial order. We can provide certificates of analysis, safety data sheets, and standard export documentation for your destination market.

4

Documentation & Shipment

We arrange export documentation including certificates of origin, packing lists, and commercial invoices. Goods are shipped from Tema Port, with lead times and minimum order quantities confirmed at the time of order placement.
