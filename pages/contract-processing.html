The Service

What Is Contract Processing?

Contract processing — also called toll manufacturing or toll processing — means you provide the formulation, specification, or raw materials, and we manufacture it for you. You retain ownership of your process and product; Elrisa provides the expertise, equipment, and facility.

This is ideal for companies that:

  • Need a local manufacturing partner in Ghana or West Africa
  • Want to test a new formulation before full-scale investment
  • Are scaling up from lab to commercial production
  • Lack local production capacity but need regional supply
  • Want to incorporate bio-based feedstocks into existing products
What We Offer

Our Contract Processing Services

Toll Manufacturing

You supply the raw materials or feedstocks, we process them into your specified product. Full confidentiality maintained. Suitable for chemical intermediates, extracts, and refined specialty chemicals.

Custom Blending & Formulation

We work with your chemists to blend, modify, or formulate chemicals to your exact specification — incorporating bio-based components such as CNSL, cardanol, vegetable oils, or other renewable feedstocks.

Pilot Batch Production

Small-scale pilot batches allow you to validate formulations, collect samples for testing, and refine specifications before committing to full commercial production. Ideal for R&D teams and new product development.

Scale-Up Support

Moving from lab scale to commercial production? We support the transition — optimising process parameters, identifying raw material sources in Ghana/West Africa, and managing the ramp-up to full production volumes.

Contract Supply & Distribution

Long-term supply agreements for consistent, scheduled delivery of chemical intermediates and specialty chemicals within Ghana and across West Africa. Reliable sourcing backed by our local agricultural supply chain.

Green Chemistry Consulting

We help manufacturers identify bio-based alternatives to petrochemical ingredients in their existing formulations — reducing carbon footprint, improving sustainability credentials, and meeting green procurement requirements.

The Process

How It Works

A straightforward four-step process — from initial discussion to product in your hands.

01

Discuss Your Requirements

Contact us with your project details — what you need produced, what raw materials you have, your target volumes, and any specific quality or specification requirements. NDAs can be signed upfront.

02

Feasibility & Proposal

We assess technical feasibility using our facility and equipment, review your formulation or specification, and provide a detailed proposal covering process, timeline, pricing, and quality controls.

03

Pilot Batch & Validation

We run a pilot batch, provide you with samples and test data for approval. Any formulation adjustments are made at this stage before scaling. You maintain full control over your IP throughout.

04

Commercial Production & Delivery

Once approved, we move to full-scale production and agree on a delivery schedule. Products are packaged to your specification and dispatched — within Ghana, across West Africa, or for export.
