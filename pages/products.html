Specialty Chemicals from Renewable Sources

Every product in our portfolio begins as an agricultural residue — and ends as a high-value industrial material.

01

Cashew Nut Shell Liquid (CNSL)

Our flagship product. CNSL is a naturally occurring phenolic lipid extracted from cashew nut shells — a versatile bio-based feedstock with exceptional chemical properties.

Applications

Friction materials Brake linings Resins Surface coatings Waterproofing Lamination

Key Properties

High thermal stability Chemical resistance Natural polymerization Bio-renewable
