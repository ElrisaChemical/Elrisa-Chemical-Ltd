Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Elrisa Chemicals Ltd ("Elrisa", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what data we collect when you visit our website, why we collect it, and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

Contact Form Submissions

The only personal information we collect is what you choose to provide when you fill out our contact form. This includes:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company or organisation name (optional)
  • Your message and inquiry details

We do not collect any information about you automatically. We do not use analytics tools, tracking pixels, or any technology that monitors how you browse our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or request
  • Provide product information, pricing, or samples as requested
  • Follow up on partnership or supplier discussions

We will never use your contact details to send unsolicited marketing messages.

3. How Your Form Data Is Processed

Our contact form is powered by Web3Forms, a third-party form submission service. When you submit the form, your data is securely transmitted to Web3Forms, which forwards it to our email inbox and does not store it beyond delivery. Web3Forms operates under its own privacy policy, which you can review at web3forms.com/privacy.

4. Cookies & Browser Storage

Our website does not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies of any kind. The only browser storage we use is a single localStorage entry that remembers whether you have accepted or declined our cookie notice. This preference:

  • Never leaves your browser — it is not sent to any server
  • Contains no personal information
  • Is used only to avoid showing the consent banner on repeat visits

You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this website.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third party, except Web3Forms as described in Section 3 above, and legal authorities if required by applicable law.

6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are delivered to our email inbox and retained only as long as needed to address your inquiry. You may request deletion of your information at any time by emailing us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Withdraw any consent you have given

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@elrisachemical.com and we will respond promptly.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies independently.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for business and professional audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we collect or use data, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to check back periodically.
